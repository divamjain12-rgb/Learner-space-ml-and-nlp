GENERAL NOTES
- TA orientation attendance is mandatory; absence can lead to a financial penalty in scholarship/support.
- Registration for second half-semester courses must be completed at the start of the semester.
- Saturdays count as valid days for both mid-semester and end-semester exam windows.
- Classes missed due to public holidays may be rescheduled on Saturdays/Sundays with Dean (AP) approval.
