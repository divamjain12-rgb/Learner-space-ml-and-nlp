MASTERS PROGRAMME (Group I category students)
- TA (Teaching Assistantship): 8 hours/week of TA duty in a course/laboratory, as assigned by the academic unit.
- RA (Research Assistantship): 20 hours/week as a research assistant in a course/laboratory, as assigned by the academic unit.
- TAP (Teaching Assistantship through Project): 8 hours/week working on the same project.
- RAP (Research Assistantship through Project): 20 hours/week working on the same project.
- FA (Fellowship Award): disbursement and continuation follow the norms of the awarding agency, or IIT Bombay's TA-category norms if unspecified.

PhD PROGRAMME
- TA: 8 hours/week TA duty in a course/laboratory.
- RA: 20 hours/week as research assistant in a course/laboratory.
- TAP/RAP: continuation is subject to serving the assigned project under the Project Investigator(s) AND maintaining satisfactory academic performance; TAP is 8 hours/week, RAP is 20 hours/week.
- FA: same as Masters — follows awarding agency norms or IITB's TA-category norms.
- FRN-TA (Foreign National TA): 8 hours/week TA duty, same as regular TA.
- FRN-FA: not specified in the information brochures.

GROUP II CATEGORY STUDENTS
Faculty members may take on additional TA/RA support beyond their assigned Group I TAs/RAs for a semester's academic activity, with permission from the Associate Dean/Dean (Academic Programmes — A/Dean AP).
Students under SW (Sponsored), EX (External), SF (Self-Financed), CT (College Teacher), PS (Project Staff), and SW IITB-Monash categories may be assigned TA duty with A/Dean AP approval; this approval must be obtained within 15 days of the start of the semester. These students receive a partial refund of tuition fees (equivalent to the Group I rate) upon submitting a certificate of completed TA duties at the end of the semester — Rs 20,000 for Masters students and Rs 22,500 for PhD students.
